Our Mission

A non-profit organisation aiming to increase access to quality STEM education for youth in underserved communities

GLOBAL GOALS NUMBER 4

- Quality Education-

Education liberates the intellect, unlocks the imagination and is fundamental for self-respect. It is the key to prosperity and opens a world of opportunities, making it possible for each of us to contribute to a progressive, healthy society. Learning benefits every human being and should be available to all. - globalgoals.org

- Why goal number 4? -

We believe that there is a lack in quality education in Thailand. There are an adequate amount of resources , but the problem lies within the distribution and execution of those resources. The government has made huge investments into education, yet no promising results are shown ( mostly in rural areas or underserved communities ).

At the end of lower secondary education, only 46% have a minimum proficiency in mathematics. As there is a clear scaarcity in STEM education, we would like to make science, technology, engineering & mathematics our main focus for our workshops.

- Our targets for this goal-

Our activities will mainly focus on these goal 4 targets:

TARGET 4.1

: Ensure that all girls and boys complete free, equitable and quality primary and secondary education leading to relevant and effective learning outcomes.
: We will host wokshops that concerns both primary and secondary students as well as making sure the content that we teach / carry out will be to the highest quality. We will deliver these resources at no cost for children in marginalized communities.

TARGET 4.5

: Eliminate gender disparities in education and ensure equal access to all levels of education and vocational training for the vulnerable, including persons with disabilities, indigenous peoples and children in vulnerable situations.
: To ensure that there are equal opportunities for all regarding education, our target audience for our workshops are children in underpriviledged households and communities (slums).

TARGET 4.6

: Ensure that all youth and a substantial proportion of adults, both men and women, achieve literacy and numeracy.
: We will highly focus on numeracy as it is one of the STEM subjects.
